## 📊 CHART SPECIFICATIONS

### Pie Charts
- **Type**: Pie
- **Data**: Categorical with percentages
- **Colors**: Multi-color palette
- **Features**: Legend, tooltips, responsive

### Bar Charts
- **Type**: Bar (Horizontal & Vertical)
- **Data**: Categorical with numeric values
- **Colors**: Single or multi-color
- **Features**: Axis labels, grid, responsive

### Line Charts
- **Type**: Line
- **Data**: Sequential with numeric values
- **Colors**: Single or multi-line
- **Features**: Trend line, fill area, responsive

### Scatter Plots
- **Type**: Scatter
- **Data**: Two numeric variables
- **Colors**: By category
- **Features**: Point size, labels, responsive

### Doughnut Charts
- **Type**: Doughnut
- **Data**: Categorical with percentages
- **Colors**: Multi-color palette
- **Features**: Center label, legend, responsive

### Radar Charts
- **Type**: Radar
- **Data**: Multiple dimensions
- **Colors**: Single or multi-series
- **Features**: Grid, axis labels, responsive
